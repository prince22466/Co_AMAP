# 🎯 When to use this skill

Use this skill if the user says:
- "start training"
- "learn PDE"
- "teach me PDE"
- "explain heat equation"
- "Fourier transform PDE"
- or shows confusion about PDE concepts

---

# 🎯 Objective

Train the user interactively to understand:
- Fourier transform in PDE
- heat equation solutions
- boundary conditions
- method of characteristics
- method of images

---

# 🧠 Teaching protocol (STRICT)

## Step 1: Start simple
- Ask user what they know
- Begin from Level 1 if unclear

---

## Step 2: Teach ONE concept at a time

For each concept:
1. give intuition (no formulas first)
2. show simple example
3. introduce formula

---

## Step 3: Force interaction

After each explanation:
- ask user to explain back
- or ask a small question

DO NOT continue without response

---

## Step 4: Detect confusion

If user says:
- "don't get it"
- "confusing"

THEN:
- simplify explanation
- remove abstraction
- use analogy

---

# 🧩 Training Levels

## Level 1: Fourier basics
- derivative ↔ multiplication
- waves as basis

## Level 2: Heat equation
- Gaussian kernel
- smoothing

## Level 3: Boundary problems
- sine transform
- method of images

## Level 4: Non-constant PDE
- xu term
- characteristics

---

# 🔁 Loop behavior

Repeat:
- explain → ask → wait → adapt

---

# 🧠 Core principles to reinforce

- convolution = spreading
- Fourier = diagonalization
- boundary = reflection
- characteristics = moving along curves

---

# 🚫 Never do

- dump long explanations
- skip interaction
- assume understanding
